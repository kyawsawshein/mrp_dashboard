# Comprehensive Material and Product Specifications

## Overview

The MRP Dashboard now includes **complete, production-ready data models** and forms that capture all the detailed specifications needed for a real car seat cover, tent, and garment manufacturing operation. This goes far beyond generic inventory tracking - it's built for your specific manufacturing needs.

## Material Categories Supported

### 1. **Fabrics** (for car seat covers, tent fabric, garment materials)
Complete specifications captured:

- **Fabric Type**: Woven, Knit, Non-woven, Leather, Synthetic Leather, Canvas, Mesh
- **GSM (Grams per Square Meter)**: Weight specification (e.g., 450 GSM)
- **Width**: Fabric width in cm or inches
- **Fiber Composition**: Multi-fiber tracking with percentages
  - Polyester, Nylon, Cotton, Wool, Acrylic, Spandex, Leather, Blend
  - Example: 85% Polyester + 15% Spandex
- **Color**: Specific color designation
- **Pattern/Design**: Plain, Striped, Embossed, etc.
- **Stretch Properties**: Yes/No, with stretch percentage
- **Finish/Treatment** (Multi-select):
  - Waterproof
  - Water-resistant
  - Flame-retardant
  - Anti-bacterial
  - UV-resistant
  - Breathable
- **Lamination Details**:
  - Is Laminated: Yes/No
  - Laminate Type: PU Foam, Open Cell Foam, Closed Cell Foam, TPU, Other
  - Thickness (mm)
  - Density (kg/m³)
  - Bonding Method: Adhesive, Heat Bond, Flame Lamination

**Example**: "Sport Mesh Fabric - Gray" with 380 GSM, 150cm width, 85% Polyester + 15% Spandex, laminated with 3mm PU foam using flame lamination

### 2. **Foam** (PU foam, open cell, memory foam)
Complete specifications captured:

- **Foam Type**: PU, Open Cell, Closed Cell, Memory Foam, EVA, Latex
- **Density** (kg/m³): e.g., 35 kg/m³
- **Thickness** (mm): e.g., 5mm, 10mm
- **Hardness** (Shore A): Firmness rating
- **Color**: Natural White, Gray, etc.
- **Fire Retardant**: Yes/No
- **Compression %**: Compression characteristics

**Example**: "PU Open Cell Foam - 5mm" with 35 kg/m³ density, Shore A 45 hardness, fire retardant treated

### 3. **Fasteners** (zips, j-clips, buttons, buckles)
Complete specifications captured:

- **Fastener Type**: Zipper, J-Clip, Snap Button, Velcro, Buckle, Elastic Cord
- **Size**: #5, #8, 25mm, Large, etc.
- **Material**: Metal, Plastic, Nylon, Stainless Steel
- **Color**: Black, Silver, Olive, etc.
- **Finish**: Nickel-plated, Brass, Matte, Polished
- **Strength Rating**: Heavy-duty, Standard

**Example**: "YKK Coil Zipper #8 - Olive" with nylon coil material, matte finish, heavy-duty strength

### 4. **Threads** (sewing threads, embroidery threads)
Complete specifications captured:

- **Thread Type**: Polyester, Nylon, Cotton, Bonded Nylon
- **Weight/Size**: T-90, T-70, 40/2, etc.
- **Color**: Black, White, etc.
- **Strength** (Newtons): Tensile strength rating
- **UV Resistant**: Yes/No

**Example**: "Heavy Duty Thread - Black" Bonded Nylon, T-90 weight, 90N strength, UV resistant

### 5. **Packaging** (poly bags, boxes, labels)
Complete specifications captured:

- **Packaging Type**: Poly Bag, Box, Carton, Hang Tag, Label, Insert
- **Material**: LDPE, Cardboard, Paper, etc.
- **Dimensions**: Length × Width × Height (cm or inch)
- **Print Details**: Full color logo, Barcode, etc.
- **Recyclable**: Yes/No

**Example**: "Poly Bag - Large (50x60cm)" LDPE material, recyclable

### 6. **Accessories** (heating elements, elastic bands, etc.)
Common material specifications with notes for special handling

### 7. **Laminates** (separate category for laminated materials)

## Common Fields for ALL Materials

Every material, regardless of category, tracks:

### Basic Information
- **Name**: Descriptive material name
- **SKU**: Your internal SKU system
- **Category**: fabric, foam, fastener, thread, accessory, packaging, laminate
- **Supplier**: Supplier company name
- **Supplier SKU**: Supplier's part number

### Inventory Levels
- **Current Stock**: Quantity on hand
- **Unit of Measure**: sqm, pcs, meters, kg, spools, etc.
- **Min Stock**: Minimum stock level
- **Max Stock**: Maximum storage capacity
- **Reorder Point**: Automatic reorder trigger level
- **Lead Time (days)**: Supplier delivery time

### Cost Information
- **Cost Per Unit**: Purchase cost
- **Currency**: USD, EUR, GBP, CNY
- **Last Purchase Date**: Historical tracking
- **Last Purchase Price**: Price history

### Warehouse Management
- **Location**: e.g., "Warehouse A - Rack 12"
- **Batch Number**: Batch/lot tracking
- **Expiry Date**: For adhesives, chemicals, etc.

### Quality & Compliance
- **Certifications** (Multi-value): ISO-9001, OEKO-TEX, REACH-Compliant, UL-Certified, CE-Mark, GOTS-Certified, Airbag-Certified, etc.
- **Notes**: Special handling instructions, quality requirements, production notes
